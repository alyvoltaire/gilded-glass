Drinks

A classic martini is a timeless cocktail made with gin and a touch of dry vermouth, typically served ice-cold in a martini glass. It's known for its clean, crisp taste and often garnished with a twist of lemon peel or an olive.

Bourbon is a quintessentially American whiskey known for its sweet, caramel-like flavor profile, aged in new charred oak barrels, and containing at least 51% corn in its mash. It's a beloved spirit with a rich history and a timeless appeal to whiskey enthusiasts worldwide.

A passion fruit martini, is a delightful cocktail featuring the tropical and tangy flavor of passion fruit juice, often mixed with vodka and a sweet liqueur like vanilla or passion fruit liqueur. It's typically served in a martini glass and is sometimes accompanied by a shot of sparkling wine on the side.
